How It Works

No guesswork.
No fluff. Just a system.

Every engagement follows the same four-step framework. It's repeatable, measurable, and designed to compound over time.

01

Audit

Week 1 — Discovery & Mapping

We start by mapping everything that exists: your current funnel, your ops, your team's workflow, your ad accounts, your website. We find every leak, every gap, every missed opportunity.

No assumptions. No templates. We build a picture of your actual business before recommending anything.

Full funnel audit — ads to close
Ops & team workflow review
Website & conversion analysis
CRM & follow-up gap mapping
Competitor & market scan
Revenue leak identification
02

Architect

Week 1–2 — Blueprint & Proposal

Based on the audit, we design the complete infrastructure blueprint. Which pillars you need first. What gets built in which order. What the machine looks like when it's done.

You get a clear roadmap — not a vague proposal, but an exact sequence of what gets built, when, and what outcome each piece unlocks.

Infrastructure blueprint design
Pillar prioritization & sequencing
Tech stack recommendation
Timeline & milestone planning
KPI & success metric definition
Budget allocation framework
03

Build

Week 2–4 — Execution

We build everything. Ads, CRM, automations, website, landing pages — all connected, all tracked, all tested before going live. Nothing launches until it's verified end-to-end.

You're kept in the loop at every milestone. No black boxes. No surprises. No "it's almost done."

Ad campaigns setup & launch
CRM pipeline configuration
WhatsApp & email automations
Landing pages & website builds
Tracking & pixel installation
End-to-end QA before launch
04

Optimize

Month 2+ — Ongoing Retainer

This is where most agencies disappear. We don't. Every month we review performance, identify what's working, fix what isn't, and plan the next improvement cycle.

Results compound. Month 3 always performs better than Month 1 — because we never stop optimizing.

Weekly performance review
Ad creative testing & refresh
Funnel drop-off fixes
Lead quality improvement
Monthly strategy sessions
Slack/WhatsApp direct access
